What We Do

Our Services

01
Wheel Alignment

Computer-aided 4-wheel alignment to correct steering angles, reduce tyre wear and keep your vehicle tracking perfectly straight.

02
Wheel Balancing

Precision dynamic balancing eliminates vibration, extends tyre life and delivers a smoother, quieter ride at all speeds.

03
Puncture Repair

Fast, professional puncture repair using quality materials. Tubes and tubeless both handled. Back on the road in minutes.

04
Tyre Change

Quick and safe tyre removal and fitting for all vehicles. Seasonal swaps and worn-out replacements done right the first time.

05
New Tyres

Wide selection of Apollo, Goodyear and Bridgestone tyres. Expert advice to match the perfect tyre to your vehicle and budget.

Tyre Inspection

Free visual and pressure check across all four tyres. We flag wear patterns, sidewall damage and pressure issues to keep you safe.
